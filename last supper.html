The Last Supper

Artist: Leonardo da Vinci

Summary:

The Last Supper is one of the most famous paintings by Leonardo da Vinci, created between 1495 and 1498. It depicts the moment when Jesus Christ reveals that one of his disciples will betray him, capturing the reactions of the apostles. The painting is celebrated for its composition, use of perspective, and emotional depth. It can be found in the Convent of Santa Maria delle Grazie in Milan, Italy.
